waveforms to determine what the circuit does, then implement it.

Primary objective: maximize performance by minimizing critical path delay.

Constraints:
- Keep combinational depth as shallow as possible.
- Prefer a single-stage mux/case structure; avoid cascaded if-else chains when possible.
- Keep control decode minimal and balanced.
- No sequential logic.
- Output only the synthesizable SystemVerilog code for TopModule.
